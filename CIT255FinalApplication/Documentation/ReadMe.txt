Instructions for use: 

Simply type into the TextBox, click ADD and you have
created your first item in your list!

You can add more, right-click on the item to either Edit (the text), 
Mark Done (Completes the item and shows your a specific date completed), 
and Delete (Deletes the item permanently). You can also view all of your
Completed Items, incase your forget what you had finished. Enjoy!
